Research

My recent work focuses on diffusion-based generative models, discrete diffusion for language, and learning policies for decision-making.

  • Diffusion language models. Studying conditional structure, parallel decoding, and joint-token compatibility in diffusion-based text generation.
  • Reinforcement learning & control. Off-policy optimization of generative policies and model-based control for robotics.
  • Foundations of generative modeling. Connections between diffusion, flow models, and autoregressive modeling.
